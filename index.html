About us

~Our Story

DILIcious foods and pastries is an elegant and sophisticated restaurant. Our journey started with the love for food and its preparation. We at DILIcious foods and pastries put in our experience and expertise to work to give you a blissful memory of a time well spent, and offer you the best of front line attention.

~Our Atmosphere

DILIcious foods and pastries offers 60 seating places (which can be rearranged for corporate events, parties, e.t.c.), carefully selected music and smart interior design to make you feel at home. DILIcious foods and pastries can host large groups too. So if you need more seats for your feast simply send us an email, or call us by phone, or visit the reception.

~Our Approach and Cuisine

Here at DILIcious foods and pastries, we work with only the best food suppliers to insure that we always have the best and always fresh ingredients. Our restaurant is noted for its excellent cuisine. On our menu, you'll find a large variety of delicacies (foreign and local) made to satisfy your taste. Our Nigerian cuisine is a taste ever to remain on your tongue, and our foreign delicacies are pleasant and inviting.
